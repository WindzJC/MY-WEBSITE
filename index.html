Results

How the project works

We start with positioning (hook, promise, audience), then build a trailer + author page that share the same message and look so every place you promote feels consistent and intentional.

What these assets do

  • Communicate the "why buy" in seconds (genre/tone + stakes or transformation)
  • Increase credibility fast (premium visuals + focused messaging)
  • Reduce drop-off (one clean page, clear CTAs, fewer distractions)
  • Keep branding consistent across social, ads, email, and listings
  • Make marketing easier with a reusable promo kit you can deploy anytime

Outcomes authors often see

  • Higher-quality clicks (right readers, not random views)
  • Stronger purchase intent (clear fit + clear promise)
  • Better conversions (less friction from interest to purchase)
  • More sales conversations (especially nonfiction/coaching)
  • A more professional launch presence that supports long-term promotion

Results vary by genre, platform, and traffic source.
