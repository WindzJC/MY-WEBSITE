Visual idea for your book

I’m Windelle JC, a freelance video editor and website designer behind Astra Productions. For the past six years, I’ve helped authors turn their books into cinematic trailers and focused author websites that move readers from scrolling to clicking “buy.”

I’ve worked with Barnes & Noble and other publishing companies, creating promotional visuals built to capture attention fast and make the story instantly felt.

Most books still rely on a small cover image and a long block of text on crowded store pages—so even strong titles can get scrolled past if readers don’t connect quickly.

I help your book make a stronger first impression: a movie-trailer-style teaser paired with a clean author site that shows the story’s hook and points readers straight to where they can buy.

Below you’ll find real trailers, a real author website, and a simple way to get a visual idea for your own book.
